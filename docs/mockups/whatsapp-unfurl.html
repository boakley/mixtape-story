How a mixtape lands in a chat.

A pasted mixtapestory.com URL unfurls into a preview card in WhatsApp. Tapping it opens the mixtape in the recipient's browser. The unfurl card is the funnel — most viewers decide whether to tap based on this one image and these three lines.

The unfurl card

Generated server-side as a 1200×630 PNG. Mosaic of album-art covers, title typeset in Fraunces, faint gradient overlay. Cached by mixtape version hash so a song add invalidates the previous image. This image is the funnel — most viewers decide whether to tap based on it.

The mixtape page

Editorial, not product-y. Story snippets are the hero — the song titles sit alongside, not above. Streaming pills route through Songlink so each viewer opens the song in their own preferred service. The "Ask about this song" button opens a wa.me deep-link to the mixtape creator.

Reference mockup. Visual direction only — actual implementation will use shadcn-svelte primitives.
